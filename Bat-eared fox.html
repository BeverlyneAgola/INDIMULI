Bat-eared fox

The bat-eared fox is another Kenyan animal that belongs to the dog family. It also feeds on insects like termites , lives alone or in small groups and has six more teeth than other foxes.

The bat-eared fox can be found in the savannahs, arid scrublands and grassy plains.It lives in countries including Kenya, Tanzania ,South Africa,Angola and Zimbabwe. The IUNC lists the bat-eared foxes as least concern and as having a stable population , though they are threatened by hunters,disease and drought
