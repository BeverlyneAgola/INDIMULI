Cheetah

One of the most recognizable animals in Kena is the Cheetah.These spoty cats each with their own unique patterns are skilled hunters, in apart because of their natural speed and agility. In fact, chettahs are the fastest mammals on earth. They can accelerate from 0-60miles per hour in just 3 seconds. Another interesting cheetah fact, unlike lions,tigers,jaguars and leopards cheetars cant roar they purr.

This cats live and hunt in open grasslands in africea. they can be found in countries including kenya, sa and naija. The international union of conservation of nature classes ceetahs as vulnerable. This means, the species is at high risk of extinsion in the wild. They're just 6517 mature cheetahs left in the wild and their population is decreasing. Cheetahs are at particulaar risk from habit loss because they need such a large range. Most cheetahs live in unprotected areas where they coe into conflict with farmers.
