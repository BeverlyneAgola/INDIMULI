Dikdik

The dikdik is a type of dwarf antelop that lives in arid regions of Eas Africa. They can live in such barren environments because they get water from the vegetation they eat. Thay also take cover in undergrowth to protect themselves from preditors, including eagles and cats. Thre are 4 types of dikdiks , but just 2 of them live inKenya. These include Guenther's dikdik and kirks dikdik.kirk's is the largest dikdik, though it's still small in comparison to other antelopes it stands at just 45 centimetres(18 inches) tall and weighs only 7.2kgs.Guenther's dikdikis smaller but has a longer snout.
